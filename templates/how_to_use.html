Platform-Specific Guides

Gemini One Pro (Google AI Studio)

Active Teacher
Step-by-Step Process:
1. Access Google AI Studio

Visit aistudio.google.com and sign in with your Google account

2. Navigate to Education Discount

Look for "Teacher Verification" or "Education Discount" option in your account settings

3. Start Verification Process

You'll be redirected to SheerID verification page

4. Copy the SheerID URL

The URL should look like:

https://services.sheerid.com/verify/[PROGRAM_ID]/?...
5. Submit to MSVERIFY

Go to Verify page, select "Gemini One Pro", paste the URL, and submit

6. Wait for Completion

Processing takes 1-2 minutes. You'll receive a notification once completed

Note: Make sure you don't close or refresh the SheerID page until verification is complete

ChatGPT Teacher K12 (OpenAI)

Active Teacher
Step-by-Step Process:
1. Access ChatGPT Education

Visit chatgpt.com/education or look for education discount in your OpenAI account

2. Select Teacher K12 Verification

Choose the K12 teacher verification option

3. Get SheerID URL

Copy the complete SheerID verification URL from your browser address bar

https://services.sheerid.com/verify/[PROGRAM_ID]/?...
4. Submit to MSVERIFY

Select "ChatGPT K12" service and paste your URL

5. Processing

Our system will automatically complete the verification form and submit it

Success Rate: 99% success rate for ChatGPT K12 teacher verification

Spotify Premium Student

Active Student
Step-by-Step Process:
1. Access Spotify Student

Visit spotify.com/student

2. Click "Get Started"

You'll be redirected to SheerID for student verification

3. Copy SheerID URL

From the SheerID verification page, copy the complete URL

4. Submit to MSVERIFY

Select "Spotify Student" and paste your URL

5. Automatic Verification

System will fill in student information and complete verification
